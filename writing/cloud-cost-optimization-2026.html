FinOps

Cloud cost optimization patterns for 2026

By Akkireddy Challa / Updated 2026

Cloud cost pressure is constant. The practical patterns gaining traction in 2026 include smarter autoscaling, aggressive use of spot/preemptible instances where safe, rightsizing via telemetry, and embedding FinOps into the development lifecycle.

Autoscaling economics

Tune autoscaling policies to balance request latency and cost; use predictive scaling where appropriate to smooth spikes without overprovisioning.

Spot and preemptible strategies

For non-critical workloads, combine checkpointing and workload partitioning to safely leverage spot capacity at large discounts.

Telemetry-driven rightsizing

Use production metrics to identify waste and suggest instance type or container size changes; automate suggestions in CI pipelines.
